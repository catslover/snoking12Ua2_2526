## üìò Game Format & Rules  

### Round-Robin Games  
- Warm-up: 5 minutes  
- Periods: 15 / 15 / 20 (stop-time)  
- Wall-clock is official; buzzer at 5 minutes remaining  
- Ties allowed in round-robin  
- **Scoring & Standings:** 2 points for a win, 1 for a tie  
- **Tie-Breakers for Seeding:**  
  1. Most Points  
  2. Most Wins  
  3. Head-to-Head Record  
  4. Goal Differential (Goals For ‚àí Goals Against, max +7 per game)  
  5. Fewest Penalty Minutes  
  6. Coin Toss  

### Semis & Medal Games  
- Same timing as round-robin  
- If tied after regulation:  
  - 5-minute **sudden-victory OT** (4 skaters + goalie)  
  - If still tied ‚Üí shootout  
- Each team gets one 30-second timeout (semis/medals only)
